Welcome to HiVE Cluster - Smart Home Interface

In the digital era we are currently living having a smart home is a must. There was time for smartphones, but now we are ready for SmartHomes.

The system enables the user to do following options

  1. Thermostat temperature set
  2. Setup Alarm
  3. Ability to remotely control all locks in your house
  4. Opening the garage within one click
  5. Ability to control lightning - all in one go or every room individual
  6. Emergency Panic Button - within one click, our team would be informed that something is wrong or doesn't work within your home

The site is very easy to navigate it contains of 6 subpages

  • Main - the site you are currently viewing.
  • House plan - plan of house with a clickable interface to turn on/off light in every room directly
  • Controls - interface to set up: thermostat for heating/cooling, garage open/close, doors lock/unlock, burglar alarm on/off
  • Emergency - within one click of button inform the team for system failure.
  • Map - using Google Map to set the location for your current home
  • Faq - Frequently Ask Question for general help/enquires
